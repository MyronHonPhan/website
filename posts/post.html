Introduction

So this is my first post. This is pretty much going to be outlining who I am, what type of content I will be posting and miscellaneous things.

A Little About Me

Starting off with a little bit about me, I’m Myron! I outlined this a little bit in my “About me” but I’m a graduate student at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD) studying dynamical systems and guidance, navigation and control (GNC).

Currently, I’m working/learning a lot of aircraft and spacecraft simulations as my primary focus. Simply because I find simulating dynamic systems to be really fun! So I will be definitely posting a lot about that in particular. Although, I’m also reading a lot of Kalman filtering and GNC algorithms for robotics applications so I will also be posting a bunch about that.

In terms of posting frequency, it will at the very least be one post a week. Some weeks might be more than one because I already document and take notes on what I learn anyways.
